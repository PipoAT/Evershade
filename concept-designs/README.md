## Design Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Gothic Sci-Fi Fusion**
   - Victorian-era architecture meets advanced technology
   - Ornate details on futuristic equipment
   - Cathedral-like spaceships and buildings
   - Classical music influences for alien factions

2. **Asymmetrical Faction Design**
   - Each faction has distinct visual language
   - Humans: Industrial, naval, military blue and gold
   - Crystal Ravagers: Organic crystal, purple glow, Victorian aristocracy
   - Future factions: Unique aesthetics to be detailed

3. **Gameplay-First Visuals**
   - Clear silhouettes for identification
   - Color-coded friend/foe/objective
   - Visual feedback for all gameplay states
   - Readable despite visual complexity

4. **Atmospheric Immersion**
   - Dynamic weather and lighting
   - Environmental storytelling
   - Audio design supporting visuals
   - Mood-appropriate color grading

## Visual Style Guide Summary

### Color Palettes

**Human Aegis Fleet:**
- Primary: Navy Blue (#1A2332)
- Secondary: Steel Gray (#5D6D7E)
- Accent: Gold (#D4AF37)
- Energy: Blue (#3498DB)
- Alert: Red (#C0392B)

**Crystal Ravagers:**
- Primary: Ethereal Purple (#8E44AD)
- Secondary: Crystal Blue (#3498DB)
- Tertiary: Pale Violet (#E8DAEF)
- Base: Dark Obsidian (#1C1C1C)

### Typography

**Primary Font:** "Aegis Gothic" (custom)
- Clean, industrial aesthetic
- High legibility at small sizes
- Slight angular design
- Used for UI and in-game text

**Display Font:** "Cathedral" (decorative)
- Gothic-inspired serifs
- Titles and logos only
- Used sparingly for impact

### Icon Design Principles

1. Simple silhouettes recognizable at 32x32px
2. High contrast (dark bg / light icons)
3. Consistent 2-3px stroke weight
4. Color coding: Blue=Friendly, Red=Enemy, Yellow=Objective, Green=Health

## Technical Specifications

### 3D Asset Standards

**Character Models:**
- LOD 0: 25,000 triangles (gameplay)
- LOD 1: 15,000 triangles (distance)
- Textures: 4K for hero characters, 2K for NPCs
- Rigging: ~80 bones, full articulation

**Titan Models:**
- LOD 0: 50,000 triangles (gameplay)
- LOD 1: 25,000 triangles (distance)
- Textures: 4K hero, 2K gameplay
- Rigging: ~120 bones with mechanical constraints

**Environment Assets:**
- Modular building pieces: 5,000-15,000 tris each
- Props: 500-5,000 tris depending on size
- Textures: 2K-4K depending on prominence
- Lightmap baking for interiors

### Performance Targets

**Siege of Rancor (RTS):**
- Frame Rate: 60 FPS target (30 FPS minimum in large battles)
- Unit Count: Up to 500 units simultaneously
- Draw Distance: 5km for space battles
- Resolution: 1080p standard, 4K supported

**Shadow War (FPS):**
- Frame Rate: 60 FPS on consoles
- Resolution: Dynamic 1080p-4K
- Players: 12v12 multiplayer
- View Distance: 1km with aggressive LOD
