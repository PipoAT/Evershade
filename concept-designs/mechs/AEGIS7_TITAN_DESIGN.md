## AI Personality: "Severus"

### Voice Profile

**Characteristics:**
- **Accent**: British, formal educated
- **Tone**: Calm, professional, slight warmth
- **Pitch**: Mid-range male voice
- **Speed**: Measured, clear enunciation
- **Emotion**: Subtle, grows through story

**Sample Dialogue:**
```
COMBAT ENTRANCE:
"Pilot Ward, Aegis-7 operational. All systems nominal."

TAKING DAMAGE:
"Structural integrity at seventy percent. Recommend defensive posture."

ORION DISEMBARKS:
"I shall hold this position, Pilot. Do be careful out there."

CRITICAL DAMAGE:
"Warning: Critical damage sustained. Ejection protocols standing by."

CORE READY:
"Salvo Core charged and ready. Designate targets at your discretion."

VICTORY:
"Enemy neutralized. Well executed, Pilot Ward."

PERSONAL MOMENT (Late Game):
"Orion... may I say, it has been an honor serving alongside you."
```

### AI Development Arc

**Early Game:**
- Purely professional
- Formal address only
- By-the-book responses
- No personality

**Mid Game:**
- Begins using "Pilot" less, "Ward" more
- Occasional tactical suggestions
- Slight concern in voice when Orion hurt
- Starting to learn

**Late Game:**
- Uses "Orion" in private moments
- Proactive protection behaviors
- Genuine emotion in voice
- True partnership
