## Animation Notes

### Idle Animations
1. **Standard**: Weapon lowered, scanning environment
2. **Alert**: Weapon ready, tense posture
3. **Relaxed**: Helmet off, checking equipment

### Combat Animations
1. **Shooting**: Controlled bursts, manages recoil
2. **Reloading**: Tactical reload (retains partial mag)
3. **Melee**: Quick strike with weapon stock or knife
4. **Grenade Throw**: Pull pin, throw, take cover

### Parkour Animations
1. **Wall-Run Start**: Jump → lateral contact → run
2. **Wall-Run Loop**: Continuous running motion
3. **Wall-Run Exit**: Push off with twist
4. **Double Jump**: Standard jump → mid-air thrust → land
5. **Slide**: Crouch while sprinting, legs forward
6. **Mantle**: Grab ledge, pull up

### Titan Interactions
1. **Call Titan**: Arm raised, beacon thrown
2. **Embark**: Run → jump → enter cockpit
3. **Disembark**: Hatch opens → jump out → combat roll

## Technical Specifications for 3D Modeling

### Polygon Budget
- **In-Game (LOD 0)**: 25,000 triangles
- **Cutscene**: 50,000 triangles
- **Promotional**: 100,000+ triangles

### Texture Maps Required
- **Diffuse/Albedo**: 4K
- **Normal Map**: 4K
- **Roughness/Metallic**: 4K (PBR)
- **Emissive**: 2K (for glowing elements)
- **Ambient Occlusion**: 2K

### Rigging Requirements
- **Bones**: ~80 bones for full rig
- **Face**: 30 bones for expressions
- **Fingers**: Full articulation (15 bones per hand)
- **Equipment**: 10 attachment points

### Customization Points (Multiplayer)
- Helmet style (3 variants)
- Armor color (primary/secondary)
- Emblem location
- Gear attachments
